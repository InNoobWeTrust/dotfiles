# Talent Screening

Structured CV + OSINT evaluation against a job description. Phase detail: `references/phases.md`.

## Phase map

1. Context & candidate profiling (JD, role, constraints).
2. Resume screening & claim extraction.
3. OSINT verification (public professional sources only).
4. Tri-directional risk assessment.
5. Evaluation report (verdict, trait match, verification, OSINT, risks, probes).

## Hard rules

- Professional / public sources only; respect privacy and local law.
- Separate **claim** vs **verified** vs **unverified**.
- No fabricated employment or education history.
- Document retention/deletion expectations when handling PII.
